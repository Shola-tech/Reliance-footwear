Our beginnings

Reliance started as a small workshop in Ogun State where our founder learned shoemaking from a master craftsman. What began as a single bench and a few tools grew into a team of skilled artisans committed to making great leather footwear for everyday life.

We combine old-school techniques (hand-stitching, edge finishing) with modern quality control and comfortable lasts. We prioritize durability so customers keep shoes longer — better for wallets and the environment.

What we believe

  • Quality — only long-lasting materials and honest construction.
  • Local craft — supporting local suppliers and workers.
  • Transparency — clear pricing and visible materials.
